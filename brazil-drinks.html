Brazilian rum and raspberry cocktail

Price: R200.00

Serving Size: around 1/32 ounce (or 1/5 teaspoon

Shake the raspberry puree with the rum and crushed ice. Pour over the crushed ice in the glass and top up with cranberry juice or mango and passion fruit juice to taste.
